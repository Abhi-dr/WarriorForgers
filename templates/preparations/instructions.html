OLQ Estimator Instructions

Welcome to the OLQ Estimator. Please read the instructions carefully before starting the exam.

  1. Duration: The exam will be conducted for 20 minutes. The timer will start once you begin the exam.
  2. Number of Questions: The exam consists of 20 multiple-choice questions.
  3. Question Format: Each question will have four options, and you need to select the correct one.

  5. Navigation: You CANNOT navigate between questions. Only "Next" button is there.
  6. Submit: Once you have completed all the questions, click the "Submit" button to finish the exam.

Note: Do not refresh the page or close the browser window during the exam, as it may result in disqualification.
